Verbs that describes states and feeling, not actions, are normally used in the present simple, not the continuous, even if we are referring to now: 
- agree
- be
- believe
- belong
- depend
- forget
- hate
- hear
- know
- like
- look loke
- love
- matter
- mean
- need
- prefer
- realize
- recognize
- remember
- seem
- suppose
- understand
- want

Possession, opinion, understanding are non-action:
- have
- think
- see
